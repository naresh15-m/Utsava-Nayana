About Us

Get to know Utsava Nayana and our passionate creator

Raghunath Malyavantham

Welcome to Utsava Nayana, where we specialize in capturing the most precious moments of your life. Founded by Raghunath Malyavantham, we are passionate about creating stunning visual stories that you will cherish forever.

With years of experience in photography and videography, we have developed a unique style that combines artistic vision with technical expertise. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and delivering results that exceed your expectations.

Based in Hyderabad, Vijayawada, and surrounding areas, we serve clients across South India for weddings, events, and commercial projects. We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients by understanding their vision and bringing it to life through our lens.
